About

KodyU is a finance companion app exclusively for Kody merchants. It gives business owners real-time visibility into their KodyCard balance, full transaction history, and the ability to manage their cards instantly — view PIN, freeze/unfreeze, check details — all from their phone.

The app also supports receipt attachment for banking reconciliation, helping merchants stay organised. Authentication is handled through Kody Universe credentials with multi-factor authentication for security.

Key Features

Real-time KodyCard balance and transaction tracking

Instant card management — view PIN, freeze/unfreeze

Receipt attachment for banking reconciliation

Kody Universe authentication with MFA

Complete transaction history and insights
