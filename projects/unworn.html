Unworn

AI-powered garment reimagining tool — breathe new life into clothes you've stopped wearing.

Flutter Gemini Clean Architecture

About

Unworn is a tool that helps you reimagine clothes sitting untouched in your wardrobe. Take a photo of a garment you've stopped wearing, and AI suggests new ways to style, alter, or repurpose it. The goal is to fight fast fashion by making what you already own feel fresh again.

Built with Flutter and powered by Google's Gemini for visual understanding and creative suggestions. The architecture follows clean architecture principles — keeping the AI layer, domain logic, and presentation cleanly separated.

Key Features

Photo-based garment analysis using Gemini vision

AI-generated styling and repurposing suggestions

Wardrobe catalog to track owned garments

Clean Architecture with clear separation of concerns

Cross-platform mobile experience via Flutter
