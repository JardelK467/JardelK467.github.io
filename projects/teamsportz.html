About

TeamSportz Play is a sports team management app for athletes, parents, and coaches. It handles everything from match and training scheduling to real-time team chat, video highlights, and wellbeing tracking. The goal is to give grassroots sports teams access to the same kind of tech that professional teams use.

Built with Flutter and Firebase for real-time data sync. The app supports multiple teams per account, parent-managed child profiles, in-app payments, and video/photo sharing — all designed to keep everyone on the same page.

Key Features

Match, training, and event scheduling with calendar sync

Real-time team chat with video, photo, and poll sharing

Game and training video replay and highlight creation

Live game and training stats

Daily wellbeing tracking — mood, sleep, fatigue, soreness, nutrition, stress

In-app team payments (one-off and recurring)

Parent-managed child profiles

Multi-team support with a single account
