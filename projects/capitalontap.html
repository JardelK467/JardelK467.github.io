About

Capital On Tap is a business credit card app designed to make managing business finances effortless. Used by over 200,000 small business owners globally, the app gives real-time visibility into spend, lets you set employee card limits, manage cards, and redeem rewards — all from your phone.

The app supports real-time spend tracking, transaction and statement management, digital wallet integration, and card controls like freeze/cancel. It's rated 4.8 on Google Play with 7,000+ reviews — a product people genuinely rely on day to day.

Key Features

Real-time spend tracking across the business

Employee spend limits and card controls

Transaction history and statement downloads

Card management — order, freeze, cancel

Rewards points redemption

Digital wallet integration (Apple Pay, Google Pay)

One-off and recurring payment management
